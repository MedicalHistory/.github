# MedicalHistory

MedicalHistory.app is a personal medical record system designed to help individuals organize medical documents and understand their health data over time.

Most people receive medical information as isolated files: laboratory reports, diagnostic summaries, prescriptions, vaccination records, and doctor's notes. These documents are usually delivered as PDFs or images from different clinics, hospitals, and laboratories. Over time they become scattered across email inboxes, cloud storage, and personal folders.

MedicalHistory.app provides a structured way to store these documents and extract meaningful health information from them.

## The problem

Personal health data is rarely structured for long-term understanding.

Patients often receive:

- blood test reports from multiple laboratories  
- diagnostic reports from hospitals  
- prescriptions and medication summaries  
- vaccination records  
- doctor’s notes and follow-up recommendations  

Each document contains valuable information, but comparing them across time is difficult when they remain as isolated files.

Understanding trends in biomarkers such as ferritin, vitamin D, glucose, or cholesterol often requires manually opening multiple PDFs and comparing numbers.

MedicalHistory.app aims to simplify this process.

---

## What MedicalHistory.app does

The application allows users to build a personal archive of medical documents and convert important information into structured data.

Typical workflow:

1. Upload medical documents  
   Users can upload laboratory reports, diagnostic summaries, prescriptions, and other medical files.

2. Extract structured information  
   The system identifies relevant medical data such as biomarkers, diagnoses, medications, and other key information.

3. Review extracted values  
   Users review extracted information before confirming it, ensuring the personal record remains accurate.

4. Build a health timeline  
   Confirmed data becomes part of a structured timeline that helps visualize changes over time.

---

## Example use cases

MedicalHistory.app is useful for people who want to:

- track blood test results across multiple years  
- monitor biomarkers such as ferritin, cholesterol, glucose, or vitamin D  
- organize medical records from different healthcare providers  
- keep personal copies of health documents in one place  
- prepare more easily for medical appointments

Instead of comparing separate PDF reports manually, users can review their health history in a structured timeline.

---

## Data ownership and privacy

Medical records are highly sensitive. The core principle behind MedicalHistory.app is simple:

Users remain in control of their own medical data.

Documents are processed only to extract structured health information. Users review extracted values before confirming them in their personal record.

The application helps organize personal medical information and does not provide medical advice or diagnosis.
